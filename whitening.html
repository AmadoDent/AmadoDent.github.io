Whitening

What is it?

Teeth whitening involves bleaching your teeth to make them lighter. It can't make your teeth brilliant white, but it can lighten the existing colour by several shades.

Who needs it?

Accidents, aging, and beverages such as tea, coffee and red wine can slowly dull the appearance of your natural white enamel, resulting in a smile that is less white. Ask your dentist if a whitening treatment may be right for you.
