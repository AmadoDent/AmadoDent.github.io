Teeth Removal

What is it?

We usually begin the procedure with a local anesthetic to try and make you feel comfortable during the appointment. We use dental tools to loosen the tooth from its socket before its removal. In the case of Wisdom teeth, they are often removed if they affect your jaw, gums and/or teeth in the surrounding area. Impacted wisdom teeth are unable to fully erupt which can lead to bacteria and food getting trapped. This can lead to decay, infection and pain.

Who needs it?

You may need a tooth removed if a tooth infection is damaging the surrounding bone or is causing you discomfort. Every patient is different, so please consult your dentist if your wisdom teeth are causing you discomfort.
